Ramen Recipe

Description

Cheap ramen noodles are a ubiquitous and affordable food option enjoyed by people around the world. Typically packaged in individual servings, these instant noodles offer a quick and convenient meal solution, especially for those on a tight budget or with limited time for cooking.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Ramen Packet
  • Chashu
  • Green Onions
  • Chilli Oil
  • Tofu

Steps to cook:

  1. Slice chashu into thin slices, chop the green onions, and cut the tofu
  2. Add ramen into boiling hot water and put in the seasoning
  3. Divide the cooked noodes into serving bowls
  4. Add the sliced chashu, chopped green onions, and tofu cubes on top of the noodles
  5. Drizzle chilli oil over the ramen for extra flavor and heat
